Muffin Trivia

Origin of Muffins

Did you know that muffins originated in England in the 18th century as a flatbread? The word "muffin" comes from the French word "moufflet," meaning a soft bread. Originally baked on a griddle, these treats were transformed in the 19th century by American bakers into the fluffy, cake-like muffins we know today. The first known muffin recipe was published in a cookbook in 1820, and since then, muffins have become a beloved breakfast staple worldwide!
